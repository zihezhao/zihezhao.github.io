About AFR Services

AFR Services, headquartered in Plano, TX, is a leading provider of mortgage related services to loan originators and servicers nationwide. Our services include Insurance Tracking, Insurance Products & Support, Flood Zone Determinations, Automated Valuation Models (AVM's), Real Estate Tax Service, and Home Equity Lien Insurance.
