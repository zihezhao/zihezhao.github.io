Price Increase Announcement

 

To: All AWIP Customers

 

Date: 8-14-14

 

Re: Price Increase Announcement

 

Dear Valued Customer,

 

Due to recent upward price pressures in raw material costs driven primarily by chemicals, All Weather Insulated Panels is forced to increase its insulated metal panel prices by approximately 3%. This will vary slightly across the full line of products, depending on type and core thickness.

 

Orders in house: All in house orders will be honored at the current accepted price for up to 120 days from receipt of order. This means that the orders must ship within 120 days from the date shown on the AWIP Sales Order Acknowledgement. If there is a problem or extended production delay, just contact your sales representative and we will review the specific order.

 

Quotations: All quotes beginning Monday, September 1th, 2013, will be based on the new price list(s). Standard AWIP sales terms will continue to apply.

 

Please be assured that AWIP will continue to work diligently to obtain the best possible prices from our vendors so that we can pass those savings on to our customers, but that we will never be willing to sacrifice quality for a lower raw material price.

 

Your continued support is greatly appreciated. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact your sales representative or customer service contact.

 

Respectfully,

 

William H Lowery

President, AWIP
