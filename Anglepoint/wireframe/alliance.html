Accenture Partner

Anglepoint partners with Accenture, a global leader in management, technology and outsourcing services, to deliver industry leading software licensing and asset management services. From license compliance to license cost reduction, procurement guidance to tool installation and set-up, Accenture and Anglepoint help customers optimize the evolving world of IT asset management.

IBM Global Partner and

SAM Provider

Anglepoint is a global IBM partner and SAM services provider. With training from IBM, Anglepoint has deep expertise in IBM software licensing, and with the IBM License Metrics Tool (ILMT). Anglepoint and IBM offer a joint client service offering related to ILMT and Software Asset Management. We have been engaged at multiple organizations, including Fortune 100, to provide ILMT, software asset management, and IBM software licensing services around the world.

IAITAM Provider Member

Anglepoint is a Provider Member with the International Association of Information Technology Asset Managers, Inc. (“IAITAM”), IAITAM is a professional association for individuals and organizations involved in IT Asset Management (“ITAM”), Software Asset Management (“SAM”), Hardware Asset Management, and the lifecycle processes supporting IT Asset Management in organizations of every size and industry across the globe.

TagVault Member

Anglepoint is a member of TagVault, a neutral not-for-profit certification authority for software tagging, primarily focused on software identification tags (as specified by ISO/IEC 19770-2) and software entitlement tags (as specified by ISO/IEC 19770-3).

 

Symantec SAM Partner

Anglepoint is a Symantec SAM partner. Having been trained by Symantec, we provide Symantec software asset management and software licensing services to clients around the world.

Anglepoint is a global Oracle Gold Partner, with deep expertise in Oracle licensing and Software Asset Management.
