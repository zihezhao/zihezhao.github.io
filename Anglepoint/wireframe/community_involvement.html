Anglepoint is committed to offering top-quality professional services, but as a responsible firm, we also strive to make a difference in the world. Giving back to the community is part of who we are and what we do.

Read about how Anglepoint is doing its best to give back to the community below.

Several Anglepoint professionals, including CEO Ron Brill, and VP Kris Johnson are thought leaders in Software Asset Management. Ron is an active member of the ISO Working Group for SAM standards 19770 (ISO/IEC JTC1/SC7/WG21) as part of the United States delegation and the US Technical Advisory Group (US TAG), currently serving as Software Publisher Liaison. As part of WG21, Anglepoint is committed to working with other professionals in this field from around the world to transfer knowledge, establish standards, and promote effective SAM for the industry as a whole.

Blood cancers are the leading cause for cancer death among children. Anglepoint is a Partner for Cancer Cures (PFCC) with the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society (LLS). We are committed to support LLS's mission to cure leukemia, lymphoma, Hodgkin's disease and myeloma, and improve the quality of life of patients and their families.

Anglepoint strongly believes in higher education and enabling the generations of tomorrow to seize their future today. By sponsoring the Association for Information Systems Student Chapter at Brigham Young University (BYU), Anglepoint enables students to pursue great opportunities.

With BYU located in a beautiful mountain campus in Provo, UT and one of Anglepoint's offices located just five minutes away, Anglepoint has been able to provide students an opportunity for growth and learning beyond the classroom. Anglepoint offers students the opportunity to broaden a wide range of professional skills from application programming and development, to real-world client engagements. Interns at Anglepoint contribute in a big way to real projects.

Visit the Careers Page to learn more about open opportunities for graduate and undergraduate students. Of course, Anglepoint also offers full-time employment opportunities in Provo, UT and around the world.
