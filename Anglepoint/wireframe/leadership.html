Ron Brill is Chairman, President and CEO of Anglepoint. Prior to joining Anglepoint he was a high-performing Partner at KPMG and served as KPMG’s global leader for Software Asset Management (SAM) services.

 

Based in Silicon Valley, Ron has over 20 years of professional experience leading global teams in highly complex and dynamic business environments. He has a proven track record of helping his clients mitigate risks and realize opportunities in managing their IT and Intellectual Property assets, as well as enable and improve their commercial relationships with customers, vendors, and business partners.

 

Ron authored several papers on SAM, and frequently speak at industry events. He also served as Expert Witness in software licensing litigation. Ron is a member of the ISO Working Group for IT Asset Management standards (19770-X) as part of the United States delegation, currently serving as Software Publisher Liaison.

 

Ron’s contract compliance and optimization experience extends to Software Licensing, Royalty, Channel, Vendor/Supply Chain, and Digital Content.

 

Ron holds MBA in Information Systems and BA in Accounting and Economics degrees. Active professional certifications include: Certified Public Accountant (CPA) licensed in California, Chartered Global Management Accountant (CGMA), Certified Internal Auditor (CIA), Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE), Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), Certified Information Security Manager (CISM), Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), Certified in Standards-based SAM Professional (CSSP), and Certified Software Manager (CSM).

 

Ron is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA), the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE), the Institute of Internal Auditors (IIA), the Information Systems Audit and Control Association (ISACA), and the Licensing Executives Society (LES), amongst others.

Ravi Kohli has over 19 years of professional experience leading a variety of complex compliance-related engagements for global software and electronics companies. As a Director in KPMG's Contract Consulting Services Practice, Ravi supervised hundreds of license compliance reviews for high tech companies. He was involved in assisting a Fortune 500 High Tech Company start its current successful compliance program and both performed and supervised numerous license reviews.

 

Other experience has included technical and project management in the field of computer information systems and security. He is proficient in Windows, Solaris, and Linux and experienced with SUN, IBM compatible computers, UNIX and VAX systems, and Macintosh.

 

Ravi has served as lead internal auditor involved in development of Governance, Risk and Compliance functions for a high tech telecommunications company based in the Silicon Valley. Previously as a Senior Consultant with a security consulting firm, he was involved in designing network architecture and implementing firewall and VPN solutions for various clients.

 

Ravi holds MS and BS degrees in Geology. His professional certifications Include: Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP), Siemens (Rolm) Certified System Administrator (SCSA), CheckPoint Certified Security Engineer (CCSE), Cisco Certified Network Administrator (CCNA), CheckPoint Certified Security Administrator (CCSA), Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), and Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA).

Kris Johnson has over 10 years of experience in IT project management, focusing the last 7 years on designing and implementing both Software Asset Management (SAM) and software license compliance programs. Prior to joining Anglepoint, Kris was a Director of Contract Compliance Services at KPMG.

 

Kris co-developed KPMG's official SAM methodology, tools, and templates, and has trained over 200 professionals globally on SAM. Kris has also helped hundreds of customers better understand their software licensing positions and how they can manage software and hardware assets more effectively through cohesive policies, processes & controls, and tools. He has helped several Fortune 500 customers achieve several millions of dollars in cost savings by managing their IT assets more effectively.

 

Kris is a frequent speaker and trainer on software licensing and ITAM within the industry. In addition, Kris understands well the frontlines of IT, having worked in various technical roles at IBM and BYU.

 

Kris led the operational development of KPMG's Software License Compliance service offering and has managed the quality execution of over 500 global software end-user license and channel compliance reviews resulting in millions of dollars of collections year over year with an average ROI of over 15:1.

 

Kris has also successfully led and trained internal and external compliance teams to perform highly-customized procedures to achieve consistent quality and results across global compliance programs. He has extensive experience in designing compliance program processes, KPIs, “fair findings” policies, and quality controls. Kris is particularly skilled in the use of data analytics for compliance red flag identification and management of performance metrics. He has led the technical design of automated entitlement tracking and reconciliation systems for multiple clients, specifically in the areas of historical entitlement reconciliation, entity profiling, support & maintenance reconciliation, and upgrade/downgrade tracking.

 

Kris holds a BA in International Politics with minor emphases in Business and Russian Area Studies degree. His professional certifications Include: Certified Hardware Asset Management Professional (CHAMP), and Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP).

Patrick Lim has over 10 years of professional experience focused on software license compliance, software asset management (SAM), IBM licensing tools administration, and LAN/WAN administration. His expertise and skills have led to tens of millions of dollars in savings and cost recovery for our clients.

 

Prior to joining Anglepoint, Patrick was an experienced manager with KPMG’s Contract Compliance Services group and has led teams of 10 – 15 staffs and junior managers servicing Fortune 100 organizations with total revenues exceeding $100 billion annually.

 

Patrick’s current role includes co-developing processes, tools, and templates for the delivery of IBM License Metric Tool (ILMT) implementation and care and feeding, IBM software licensing analysis and reconciliation, and SAM assessment and process improvement. Most recently, Patrick is leading a team of professionals in managing upwards of hundred software license analysis and SAM engagements for a major software vendor.

 

Patrick holds a MBA from Brigham Young University Marriott School of Business. His professional certifications include: Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP) in Software Asset Management, Certified Software Manager (CSM), Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA), and Cisco Certified Network Administrator (CCNA).

Paul has been working for 8 years in technology and intellectual property. His focus has been to support clients in managing their contractual relationships to protect their Intellectual Property in doing so drive improved customer to partner relationships between his clients and subsequently their clients. He also specialises in helping customers improve their processes and implementing toolsets in order to gain better control of their licensed intellectual property.

 

Paul manages multiple contract compliance and software optimisation teams to support fortune 500 clients in Software and Chip manufacturing and has compliance experience in Software, Channel, Royalty, Digital and Procurement. Paul also has extensive experience in helping his clients to implement Software Asset Management (SAM) services.

 

Paul is a licensing enthusiast who enjoys supporting his clients to improve their management of licenses through the implementation of end-to-end processes supported by data and tools.

 

Paul holds a BSc (Hons) degree in Psychology and has extensive SAM certifications (including MCP, CSM, FAST) and other SAM tool vendor certifications.

Jason has over twelve years of experience specializing in software license management, software license compliance audits, software asset management (SAM) implementation, IT asset management (ITAM) implementation, software and hardware contracts, software license procurement, and software planning and deployment. Prior to joining Anglepoint, Jason has held several leadership positions within IBM Global Services as well as with a global re-seller SoftwareONE all related to software asset management consulting.

 

His specialty is in software asset management program management, publisher audit support and program implementation for large companies. He has experience in software asset management in the largest technology outsourcing company in the world (IBM) as well as software asset program/audit management for a customer with over 155,000 IT assets. He has helped numerous Fortune 500 companies obtain cost savings of over $100 million through publisher audit support and asset program management.

Jason holds a BS from the Monfort College of Business at The University of Northern Colorado in Business

 

Administration with an emphasis in Finance. He is also a Microsoft Certified Partner (MCP) in Software Asset Management (SAM) and System Center Configuration Manager (SCCM) as well as a Certified Software Manager (CSM).

Kevin Rieske has 7 years of professional experience focused on software asset management, license management, and licensing tools administration. His experience working on global projects have led to hundreds of millions of dollars saving or revenue recovery for our clients. His software licensing expertise in a number of software publishers allows for optimizing software asset management processes which help clients to reduce financial/operational risks.

 

Prior to joining Anglepoint, Kevin was a manager in KPMG’s Contract Compliance Services practice where he oversaw a team of service professionals serving multiple Fortune 100 corporations. He has strong data analysis and business intelligence skills.

 

Kevin’s current role includes SAM assessments and process improvements for clients as well as the installation and configuration of SAM tools to help clients make informed business decisions on their software portfolio needs and direction. Most recently, Kevin is leading a team of professionals that are integrating various SAM tools to be able to obtain accurate and complete software inventories and help companies manage data center migration projects as well as hardware refresh and virtualization projects.

 

Kevin holds a Master of Information Systems Management from Brigham Young University. His professional certifications include: Certified Software Manager (CSM), Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA).

Steven has seven years of contract risk consulting experience, focused primarily on supplier/vendor compliance, royalty inspections, and downstream channel compliance. His expertise involves working with manufacturers, suppliers, distributors, licensees, and other channel partners located in more than 15 countries around the world. Many such projects result in revenue recoveries, significant client ROI, and increased channel visibility. He is adept at identifying financial/operational risks and process improvements, as well as assisting clients in optimizing business partner relationships. He also has strong data analysis skills, extensive international experience, and speaks Mandarin Chinese.

 

Prior to joining Anglepoint, Steven was a high performing Manager in Deloitte & Touche’s Contract Risk and Compliance (CRC) practice where he oversaw a team of professionals serving some of Silicon Valley’s largest technology companies. Steven’s clients included multiple Fortune 500 corporations in various industries such as consumer electronics, networking hardware, semiconductor, OEM/ODM manufacturing, patent pool licensing, and pharmaceuticals. Representative project experience includes royalty compliance, supply chain compliance inspections, distributor/reseller compliance inspections, and Most Favored Customer/Nation (“MFC” or “MFN”).

 

Steven holds a B.S. in Corporate Finance from Brigham Young University and is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP).
