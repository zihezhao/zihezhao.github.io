All my photography is shot on high end digital equipment. Every image that leaves my studio goes through an extensive mastering process which includes enhancement, retouching, and editing. All finished images ordered from the shoot are delivered digtially and come with a full copyright release, so the images are yours to keep, post, and print wherever you like. I do offer high end printing in house, as well as expedited services to fit your needs. All my rates adjust specifically to what you need, so you never have to pay for anything you don't want.

 

Prices for headshots and portraits start out at $350. It includes the base cost of the shoot and photographing two different looks. It also includes your favorite image mastered and each additional you choose past the first is an additional $75. The price per image goes down when you order 10 images or more.

 

There is a $100 deposit to book a basic shoot which credits towards your end balance.

 

Your depsoit is refundable with 96 hours notice from the time of the shoot and is reschedule-able with 48 hours notice.

 

Location work is an extra charge.

 

Wedding and event photography prices vary depending on services needed and time, so just give me a call or request a quote and I will be happy to get you a detailed price. All my weddings include a custom made leather flush mount coffee table album. These albums are amazing and are on display in my studio.
