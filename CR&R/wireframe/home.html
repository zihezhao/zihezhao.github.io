CR&R Waste and Recycling Services

From waste collection to recycling, and everything in between.

Emptying your trash can curbside or at your business are just a couple of ways we help keep the communities we serve cleaner, safer, and more efficient. But at CR&R, we’re proud to offer so much more for Southern California residents and businesses throughout the West. Take a look at the many services we offer and see how we can work together to serve you best.

Services include: 3 barrel system (trash, recycle & green waste) weekly service, Additional Recycle or Green Waste barrels, Additional trash barrels with charge, ADA Walk-in Service, Barrel Repair, Missed Pick Up Collection, Christmas Tree Collection, Bulky, Item Collection, and E-Waste Collection

Services include: Solid Waste bin service with weekly service, Automated barrel service, Multi Family Waste Processing bin service with weekly service, Locking Lids, Bin liners, Christmas Tree Collection, Dump and Clean Up, Additional Pick Up, and Roll Out Service
