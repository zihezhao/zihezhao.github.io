Today’s networks are geographically distributed, often requiring travel to the remote locations. The Network Operations Center (NOC) typically has advanced diagnostics and analysis systems capable of pinpointing problems and corrective actions. The problem is these expensive systems are difficult to move and too expensive to deploy at remote locations.

 

If it’s expensive to take the equipment and technicians to the remote traffic, why not take the remote traffic to the equipment and technicians? Enter the IP Fabrics TunnelBox. TunnelBox is a small, low cost remote traffic access device that can be deployed remotely and controlled from a central location to bring specific IP streams of interest to the NOC or central IT location where the issue can be diagnosed and identified quickly. This reduces travel and employee expense and greatly reduces diagnosis time and effort with the additional benefit of faster customer response.

 

Imagine if a subscriber or employee complains of bad service. Simply program the TunnelBox at the remote location serving that subscriber to deliver the IP stream for that subscriber from your local location. The TunnelBox will tunnel the IP stream from the remote location to your local TunnelBox where it will be delivered and passed to the appropriate test and diagnostics equipment.
