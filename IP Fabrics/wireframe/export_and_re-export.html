Export and Re-Export

General Export Information

 

The IP Fabrics products are subject to the export control laws administered by the United States Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS). Their Export Administration Regulations (EARs) provide information on a wide variety of export restrictions and must be consulted if you are planning to export or re-export our equipment or software.

 

DeepProbe Information

 

DeepProbe™ and associated software modules are subject to US export controls and are classified under ECCN 5A002A1. DeepProbe™ products are authorized for export under provision (b)(1) of License Exception ENC (740.17) as CCATS G136966. DeepProbe can be exported without delay to all countries (excluding Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Sudan and Syria) with No License Required (NLR) (eligible for license exception ENC).

DeepSweep Information

 

IP Fabrics has not applied for export classification for DeepSweep™. The primary purpose of DeepSweep™ is to provide CALEA compliance for United States service providers. DeepSweep™ contains mediation function software implementations of the ATIS Standards body T1.IAS and T1.678 interface standards for Voice over IP (VoIP) and broadband internet intercept. DeepSweep™ also has a software implementation option for the CableLabs© Cable Broadband Intercept Standard (CBIS). IP Fabrics will not export DeepSweep™ nor will IP Fabrics sell to United States companies planning to re-export the DeepSweep™ without IP Fabrics first going through the export classification process for the DeepSweep™ product and associated software.

 

Entities wishing to export or re-export our products are advised to seek their own legal counsel and to consult the US Department of Commerce BIS regulations referenced above.

 

Export Reporting Requirements

 

Annual reporting is required for exports under License Exception ENC 740(B)(1) to all destinations other than Canada. Exporters must include for each item, the Commodity Classification Automated Tracking System (CCATS) number G136966 and the product name and model number of the item(s) exported (or re-exported from Canada), the manufacturer name, the ECCN number for the product, authorization type (ENC), and the item type.
