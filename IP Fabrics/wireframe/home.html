Curtis Schwaderer, President & CEO

 

Curtis Schwaderer is a communications industry visionary with a track record of identifying disruptive technologies and their timely application to solve real-world problems. Curtis has over 25 years of experience in the communications industry and has held a variety of technical leadership and management positions at RadiSys Corporation, Microware Systems, and E-Systems Raytheon prior to co-founding IP Fabrics. Curtis holds two patents in communications software, is a frequent speaker at industry events, and holds Bachelor of Science and Master of Science degrees from Iowa State University.

Charles Liu, Principal Software Lead

 

Charles Liu provides project-level oversight and leadership in the day-to-day software development activities at IP Fabrics. Charles has over 15 years of experience in the industry, having concentrated on communication protocols and holds two related patents. Charles has a deep understanding of many application and network protocols. Prior to IP Fabrics, Charles held key software development position in Oresis Communications and GE Interlogix.

Rick Robino, Operations & Sales Director

 

Rick Robino oversees pre- and post- sales, installation, test & validation, and customer service. Rick has been working at some kind of Internet-related enterprise continuously since 1996, starting out as a Unix System Administrator. During that time he has held a variety of key positions in the Engineering, Operations and Sales departments of several ISPs and carriers, including Senior Manager of Internet Operations at GST Whole Earth Networks and Senior Sales Engineer at IP Applications.
