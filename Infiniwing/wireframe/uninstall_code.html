If you have a password protected computer, the Terminal will ask you to use your password.

 

Type [delete] a few times to clear the typing area of any accidental letters or symbols. Your password will not show as you type it into the screen.

 

If you think you have made a mistake repeat [delete] and try again until you’re sure of what you wrote.
