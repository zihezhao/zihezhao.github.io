OUR MANAGERS

 

Our Neurosport managers provide care to people of all ages who have issues relating to physical injuries resulting from, for example, back and neck injuries, sprains/strains and fractures, arthritis, burns, amputations, stroke, multiple sclerosis, conditions such as cerebral palsy and spina bifida, and injuries related to work and sports. Our managers maintain strong interpersonal communication with their patients through clearly explaining, in detail, the results of their latest evaluations through diagnosing movement dysfunction as well as using interventions to treat patient/clients. Interventions may include therapeutic exercise, functional training, manual therapy techniques, assistive and adaptive devices and equipment, and physical agents and electrotherapeutic modalities.
