The Steel Source Construction Company has manufactured our American made MX SAMPELERS in California since 2000. Our affiliate had begun improving upon the original design (product designed and in use since 1966) streamlining the manufacturing and coating process in the early 1990's.

An expanded product line of water related products such as pipe (PVC and HDPE) valves and Fittings,as well as washers and dryers, sinks, kitchen equipment, water heaters and much more are also represented.

 

A drive to serve our clients with whatever their projects requires, lead to assisting the US Military prime contractors, such as the Fluor Government Group, KBR and DynCorp International, to build forward operating bases across the globe (see the LOGCAPIV project) . Our drive to support our customers with best value and superior customer service has required us develop distributorships with top manufactures of building supplies and operation materials.

About Steel Source Construction

Established in 2000 Steel Source began manufacturing the MX Sampler for cities, municipalities and water service companies.

With local and minority subcontract requirements in demand, manufactures and contractors began reaching out to SSC to carry their products and work with its teams to subcontract portions of projects set aside for Small Disadvantaged Business Enterprises.
