Water Sampling Station

Steel Source Construction > Water Sampling Station

For over 35 years the MX Sampler has been used in water quality sampling. They pay for themselves many times over through consistent and timely results with minimized labor.

 

MX Samplers are an impressive representation of priority on water quality. Esthetics make consumers receptive and accepting of units they may come in contact with.

FEATURES:

 

Heavy duty construction for longevity. Installation already compatible with system. Secure lock & weather proof sealing. Safety heights of approx. 3′-10″.

 

Application

 

• Demand monitoring.

• Water Quality Programs.

• Customer Service.

• Water Treatment.

 

Operation

 

• Plumbs into main.

• Inside shelf for housing chemicals and other assistance.

• Easy on/off valve.

 

SPECIFICATIONS

 

Material:

Sheet and plate, carbon steel, prime quality. More durable than aluminum.

 

• Sheet ASTM A 569

• Plate A 36

• Pipe ASTM A120

 

Housing fabricated with all hardware, dressed welds for clean appearance and painted.

 

Finish

The entire unit is sandblasted to commercial white. Then, one coat of gray primer and .3 mils. of fusion bonded epoxy (powder coat) are applied. Many colors are available.
