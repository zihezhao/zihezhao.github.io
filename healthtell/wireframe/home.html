The Platform: Antibodies and Real-Time Monitoring

For the past 150 years disease diagnosis has focused on biomarker discovery; blood based such as CA 125, PSA, and image based such as mammograms and CT Scans.  While each offer some value in the diagnostic continuum, it has long been known that the high false positives and negatives and low specificity and sensitivity lead to unnecessary extended patient surveillance and invasive procedures.  HealthTell introduces an unprecedented new platform, which measures an individual’s antibody binding pattern to a diverse peptide array indicating the real-time presence or absence of disease.
