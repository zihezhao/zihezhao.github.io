Scientist/Sr. Scientist

 

Location: Chandler, AZ Type: Full Time Min. Experience: Mid Level

 

This Scientist/Senior Scientist will engage in all aspects of HT’s process development including experimental design and execution, data evaluation and method/technology development.  The successful candidate will have exceptional analytical and problem solving skills and a track record of applying those skills to solve complex multidisciplinary challenges in an industrial setting.  The successful candidate will be responsible for performing individually and also within a highly collaborative team driving toward applied research goals with aggressive timelines that are required to bring our novel product to market.

 

Responsibilities:

 

Develop processes and methods for the production and characterization of high density peptide arrays.

Design and execute experiments to address key procedural aspects of the manufacturing technology.

Obtain, analyze, and manage large data sets typically associated with microarrays.

Refine and optimize procedures to deliver the highest quality results.

Interact closely with the management team and regularly present findings.

 

Skills, Knowledge and Abilities:

 

Must possess contemporary knowledge of peptide chemistries and associated analytical techniques.

Experience with high throughput methodologies is desired.

Experience with microarray technologies and/or multiplexed, solid-phase molecular assays is beneficial.

Must be an innovative thinker with excellent problem solving and analytical skills.

Ability to effectively communicate team plans, progress, and results through written and spoken reporting mechanisms is essential.

 

Required – Education and Experience:

 

Ph.D. degree in Chemistry, Analytical Chemistry, Biochemistry, or related fields of study.

Three or more years of industrial experience.
