My vision for Lake Life and Beyond came about by a desire to find out what was going on all over Norris Lake. Every resource or site I viewed took me to just one specific area. I wanted to know what was available to me and what differentiated each area from another.

As I started exploring, I discovered just how unique each area was, both geographically and culturally. I wanted to share my experiences and discover others. In the almost 20 years I have been living here, I feel I have barely made a dent, but every day is a new adventure…

This website is my leap of faith… I want to keep exploring and look forward to learning of your On and Beyond discoveries as well… Here’s to Great Adventures and Beyond…

 

Carole Warren

CEO
