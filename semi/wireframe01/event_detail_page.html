SEMI Pacific Northwest Breakfast Forum - Wafers to Wall Street: Emerging Market

Friday, May 01, 2015



RA4 - Ronler Acres 4 2501 NW 229th Avenue, Hillsboro, OR 97124





7:30 AM-11:00 AM

Hosted By

Sponsored By

IMAGE OF EVENT

Join industry colleagues on Friday, May 1, 2015, for a stimulating discussion on the outlook for the semiconductor industry, including opportunities and challenges presented by end market drivers. Our panel of industry and Wall Street experts will discuss the latest technology trends and the impact of emerging technologies (Internet of Things, Cloud/Mobile Computing, and others) on regional and global economies.

 

The role of captive investor group including deal selection, deal considerations and in advancing strategic goals of the corporation will also be discussed.  SEMI Wafers to Wall Street will also explore trends taking place in the industry from economic, market, technology, and manufacturing perspectives. 

 

The SEMI Pacific Northwest Breakfast Forum provides valuable industry information and is a great opportunity to network with your colleagues and meet other local industry executives, attendees and presenters. Stay in touch . . . and plug in!
