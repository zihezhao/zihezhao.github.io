Our exhibitors do experience misrepresentations from organizations claiming to be SEMICON West service providers. Directory list scams, particularly: FairGuide and ExpoGuideScam are not associated with SEMI or SEMICON West.

DECEPTION ALERT

Unofficial Lists - please be aware

SEMI has learned that an organization called GainConference1 claims to have the 2014 SEMICON West Attendee List - and is trying to sell this list to you. This organization is in no way affiliated with, or endorsed by SEMI. We have purchased a copy for review and the list is less than 1% accurate! The ONLY way exhibitors can obtain access to the SEMICON West attendee list is through the Convention Data Services (CDS) list rental service. Exhibitors who receive solicitations from GainConference1 or any other company indicating they have a SEMICON West Attendee List should contact SEMICON West Show Management immediately. SEMI takes every measure to protect our customer's data, and will take appropriate actions to stop the sale of these unofficial lists.
