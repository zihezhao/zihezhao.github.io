SEMICON West is the flagship industry exposition for the semiconductor supply chain— bringing together buyers and sellers of equipment, materials, software, services and related products from all over the world.

It's a multi-faceted event with exhibits, a conference, free technical programs, exciting displays, forums and business programs. Explore what’s new in electronics innovation, and examine the impact of these innovations on microelectronics manufacturing at SEMICON West.
