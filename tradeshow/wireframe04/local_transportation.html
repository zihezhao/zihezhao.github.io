SuperShuttle

SuperShuttle offers 24-hour transportation service to all downtown locations. 

Ride SuperShuttle to and from San Francisco International Airport or Oakland International Airport.

Call 1.650.246.8942 (toll free 1.800.258.3826) or reserve your service.

Taxi Services

Taxi fare from SFO to downtown San Francisco is approximately $35.00 one way.

 

Taxi pick-up is located on the lower level, outside the baggage claim areas.

 

Phone number for local taxi companies for service around San Francisco can be found in the Local Information section on the SEMICON West Mobile App.
