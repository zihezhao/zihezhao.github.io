VISA ASSISTANCE FOR INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL

Every international visitor should check with his or her local consulate regarding visa and entry regulations for United States.

If you need a travel visa, begin the visa application process now! Wait times for a visa interview can be several weeks or more in many countries, especially during peak travel periods. With this in mind, we strongly urge you to place your request for a letter of invitation to attend SEMICON West 2015 as soon as possible. See links below for more information about Visa and passport requirements for travel to the United States.

Image

460х260

Visitors to the U.S. from visa-waiver countries are required to register online, screened and authorized for entry. The Electronic System for Travel Authorization (ESTA) was ordered by the Department of Homeland Security to identify visitors who might be a threat. Authorization is good for two years or until one's current passport expires. For more information contact your local consulate regarding visa and entry regulations for United States.

Certain countries such as China, Vietnam and Russia may require a hand signed letter of invitation from SEMI in order to process travel requests. During the registration process we ask if a letter of invitation is needed and if a hand signed document is required.  Attendees from less strict countries can download a PDF generated form in the Registration Resource Center.  Attendees requiring a hand signed document will be contacted individually to arrange.

VISA INTERVIEW TIPS

Request your letter of invitation, and make your appointment for a visa interview early! Wait times for a visa interview can be several weeks or more in many countries, especially during peak travel periods. With this in mind, we strongly urge you to place your request for a letter of invitation to attend SEMICON West 2015 as soon as possible. These helpful tips may make your visa interview process easier:
